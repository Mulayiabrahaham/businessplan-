About the Author - James Acire-Arweny

James Acire-Arweny is a highly experienced business consultant, author, and speaker. Originally from Acholi-land in Northern Uganda, James has spent his career empowering entrepreneurs to navigate the challenges of starting and growing a business. With a solid foundation in banking, accounting, and taxation, James has helped many businesses achieve financial independence and long-term success.

After serving as a domestic banker in Uganda for 4 years, James moved to the United Kingdom to further his education. He spent 12 years working with HB Accountancy as a technical adviser, providing private clients with expert advice in finance and business management. James has since transitioned into the public sector, where he continues to support businesses with a focus on customer service management, finance, and taxation.

In his book Business Plan Writing Simplified, James shares his extensive knowledge and experience to help aspiring business owners write professional, effective business plans that attract investors and ensure long-term success.
